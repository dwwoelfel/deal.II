deal.II Email Portal

This page summarizes your possibilities of establishing contact to other deal.II users and to the deal.II authors. We are sorry if all this looks a little complicated, but since public email addresses are abused too often, we want to protect all members of our mailing lists.


Users

All deal.II users (and everybody interested) can enter themselves into the deal.II mailing list.

  • Join the mailing list

    If you want to receive comments of other users and react to them, you should join the mailing list. To do this, don't write to the list.

    Write email to list-server at dealii.org. Your message body should contain the line 

      subscribe deal
  • Unsubscribe from the list

    You can decide to leave the mailing list whenever you want. Write to list-server at dealii.org with a message body 

      unsubscribe deal
  • Help on the mailing list

    If you have problems subscribing or unsubscribing, write an email to list-server at dealii.org with a message body 

      help
  • Write to the mailing list

    Send an email to dealii at dealii.org. Please, use a significant subject line, such that everybody can identify your message. Note that you must be subscribed to the list before you can send a message!

  • Reading archives of the mailing list

    All mails that have been sent through the mailing lists are archived and can be read from the mailing list archive page.


Authors

Everything you consider a personal matter between you and the authors (like flaming them on missing items) should be sent to either of
  • Wolfgang.Bangerth at dealii.org
  • Guido.Kanschat at dealii.org
  • Ralf.Hartmann at dealii.org
Everything, that is not directly related to the library is to be considered personal in this respect!
