We see that, as expected, on each refinement step the two solutions coincide. The difference measured in time of treating each face only once (second version of the DG method) in comparison with treating each face twice within a nested loop over all cells and all faces of each cell (first version), is much less than one might have expected. The gain is less than 10% on the last refinement steps.
