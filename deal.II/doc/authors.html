deal.II Credits

The deal.II project was initially started by members of the Numerical Methods group at the University of Heidelberg, Germany but has since become a global, open source project. The current maintainers of the library are:

  • Wolfgang Bangerth, Texas A&M University, TX, USA
  • Timo Heister, Texas A&M University, TX, USA
  • Guido Kanschat, Texas A&M University, TX, USA and Universität Heidelberg, Germany

In order to get in contact with them, send email to authors at dealii.org.

History

deal.II draws from some ideas which were first implemented in the predecessor library, DEAL, developed by Guido Kanschat, Franz-Theo Suttmeier, and Roland Becker. This library is now being maintained by Franz-Theo Suttmeier at the University of Siegen, Germany.
